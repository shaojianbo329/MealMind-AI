# 10 简历项目描述

## 中文简历版

**MealMind AI｜AI 外卖商家经营助手｜AI 产品经理个人项目**

- 面向外卖中小商家经营场景，设计一款基于订单数据、用户评价与营销数据的 AI 运营决策助手，覆盖经营诊断、差评归因、营销建议和客服回复等核心场景。
- 独立完成用户痛点分析、竞品分析、功能架构设计、PRD 文档、Agent 工作流设计、Prompt 设计和数据指标体系搭建。
- 设计“数据分析 Agent、评价洞察 Agent、营销策略 Agent、客服话术 Agent”多 Agent 协作流程，支持商家通过自然语言提问获得经营诊断报告。
- 构建模拟订单、评价和营销数据集，并使用 Streamlit 搭建基础 Demo，展示经营看板、差评归因、营销分析和 AI 诊断流程。
- 设计产品使用、AI 质量、业务效果和风险控制四类指标，北极星指标为 AI 经营建议采纳率。

## 英文简历版

**MealMind AI | AI Product Manager Personal Project**

- Designed an AI-powered operation assistant for food delivery merchants, covering business diagnosis, review analysis, marketing strategy generation, and customer service support.
- Completed user pain point analysis, competitive analysis, feature architecture, PRD, Agent workflow design, Prompt design, and data metrics framework.
- Designed a multi-agent workflow including Data Analysis Agent, Review Insight Agent, Marketing Strategy Agent, and Customer Service Agent.
- Built mock datasets for orders, reviews, and marketing campaigns, and developed a Streamlit demo to showcase dashboard, review analysis, marketing analysis, and AI diagnosis flows.
- Defined product usage metrics, AI quality metrics, business impact metrics, and risk control metrics, with AI suggestion adoption rate as the north-star metric.
